History

Amber was founded in 2003 to present the Cornell community both traditional and contermporary Chinese dance. Right now we have ___ active members and are continuning to grow. Every year, not only do we bring many wonderful performances to Cornell events, we also host our annual showcase -- Rhythms of China.

Our Purpose

Amber's purpose is to spread the rich hertiage of Chinese culture and the beauty of Chinese dance to the public. We hope members can both perfect their dance skills and grow as an individuals. We hope that individuals learn Chinese dances through regular practice and perforamnces. Anyone interested can apply to join us now.
