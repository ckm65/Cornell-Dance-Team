Thank you for being interested in applying for Amber E-Board! We are excited to read you application! Before you fill out the form, here are some descriptions for each position:

President: You will be overseeing the entire club: workshops, performances, social events, etc. You will plan out the schedule for each semester, consult with senior advisors, and organize E-Board meetings.

Publicity Chair:Design and photography skills are highly encouraged. You will also need to take care of Amber's Facebook and Wechat account.

Treasury:You will submit budget planning forms, allocate funding, and take care of reimbursement.

Logistics Assistant:You will be in charge of ordering and organizing costumes. You are also responsible for cosmetics during performances.

Social Chair:You will represent the club and reach out for collaboration. You are also in charge of internal social events.
